How to buy:

Click on the "Buy Ukraine War NFT" button and go to the OpenSea platform

Input money on the stock exchange to buy cryptocurrency for NFT trading

Transfer these coins to the marketplace and join the auction

At the end of the auction, you get an NFT in exchange for your cryptocurrency

We withdraw the cryptocurrency you traded for the NFT and distribute it: to the Armed Forces of Ukraine, for medicine, to the refugees of war and for other needs.
